Sakkhraba eseisingsingduda eina khwaidagi pamna nattraga pamdana sakkhiba esei karambano, ei khangde. Chatkhiba concert singdagi khwaidagi nungaikhiba nattraga nungaikhidaba concert, ei haiba ngamde. Atheeba/fajaba, Nungaiba/Nungaitaba, babkna lepkadabani, eina lepkadaba natte. Eigi oina karambano haiyu hanglaklabadi, madugi paokhum ei leite. Fajei khanna sakkhraba eseising, nungai khanduna chatkhraba concert sing karambana karambano khandokpa ngamfam thokte; loina fajakhi, loina nungaikhi. Award mari(4) fangkhre. Eigi eseibu tabiraga neinabiduna, sakkhangbiduna pukning thougatpirakpa maduna henna eingonda maru oiri. Hayeng wahong fangbadagi ngasi lamkhunu fangbana henna fei haiba paoroudu, TAPTA dadi enna-teinei; fangdrabasu keisu kaide, hayenggidudi kanna hotnaduna ngaigani. Ei, esei mayektagi seihek seidana henna maru oina khalli. Lata Mangeskar, Kishor Kumar….Scorpion, Eagles…aphaoba/aphaobi kayasing asina sakpa eseisingda, marol-mari lousanba ngamdana, artha sukkhang khangdana fajei haiduna tanaribanina, fajaribadu seihek-seidaduda oifam thok-ee. Karamna esei sembage haibadudi pnbada adukki matik lui. Kaka Gandhadagi matamdagi SODDCA gi matam 1971-1996, sanglaba chahi kunmanga(25yrs) asida eigi kokta hanjanba mayamdu chingthoklaga sijinnaba oibana, chingthokfam machin yammi. Amakhak pallamba tarabadi, seihek-seida semge hairabadi chakhao hanghanba tai; lamlabadi wakhal thok-ee, puk thallabadi talli. Awards and Achievements 1ST CREATIVE MUSIC AWARD by The Office of the Dean of Students Welfare, MU and GP Womens’ College and Manipur AIDS Control Society (MACS) on 2002 ROOP RAAG AWARD on LYRICIST, SINGER & COMPOSER by Roop Raag 43rd Anniversary Celebration Committee on 2003 YOUTH ICON AWARD by Manipur University Research Club (MURC) on 2003 SINGER OF THIS YEAR, 2014 by Sheirengba Singgi Apunba Lup (SHAL) on 2014 INSPIRATIONAL SINGER OF MANIPUR by Manipur University Students’ Union (MUSU) on 2019 Early days of Tapta (1960s-1990s) Jayanta, lairaba emungda pokpa oibana mahakna eseida, kalada ngaojaba adu mahakki emunggi meeoisingna support toubiramde. Adubu amata pokchaba machanupa oirubana, mahakki apamba aningbada thoina wa haibasu ngamlamde. Asigumba awaba kayamasu thengnarakkhi, mahakna eseigi lamda khongdarakpasida. Mahakki eseigi lamda pusinbirakkhiba asi, mahakki laioikhraba makhura Gandhadani. Makhura asina mahakpu esei sakpa tambirakkhi amadi tabla thinba tambikhi. Mahakna magi makhura Gandhadagi magumda Likkol sanabada mahakpu esei saknaba neklak-e. Machanupana mike ta esei sakpadu uraga haraojarasu, mama maps aneemaknadi machanupana eseigi lamda khongdabadi pamlamde, adubu mahakki mache aneenadi asukki matik pukning thougatpirammi. Mahakki makhura Gandhadana leikhidraba matungda, mahakki leikhidraba cousin O. Thoibana mahakpu stage ta puthoknaba kanna hotnabikhi, mahakki mapanna ngasidi Jayantana meeyamgi thougal touba ngamba meesak ama oiraklibasisu. Ngaisisu Jayantana, laioikhraba cousin Thoibagi esei amadi esei mayek kaoba leitri – “Shet-pi-ro Ema laan-mee-gi phijet, Tambi-ro Baba nong-mei-kappa”, “Leibak-lei ho leibak-lei taibang taiyon-nabi leibak-lei”, “Ebungo-macha lao-ne nangbu chak-thongbi saan-na-se”. O. Thoibana mahakpu All India Radio (AIR), Imphalda Anganggi thouramda aneerak meetou tamduna sakpa esei saknabagi tanja pibikhi, maduda mahakna first tarakpana mahakki eseigi lamda mai onsannaba hotnabada chaoraba thouna ama pibikhi. Tapta at Guwahati Graduation loisallaba tungda mahak Eseigi lamda mapung fana khongdaba houre. Mahakna Guwahatida Post Graduation touringeida Guitar ama leirami. Ahing nungthil kangdana khongba sannabagadi Yumgi Yumbunafao taranithoirak (12) tanthoklakkhi hai. Mahakna Guitargi string singdu sannaduna Strum touba tamnaba hotnakhi. Matamduda Ranjittagi tips louduna heinaba hotnarammi. Adubu Ranjitna mahakpu strumming touba tambibadu pamdaba matoududa, mahakna Ranjittagi henGe khankhi. Ranjitna tambiyu yaramdabaduna, Jayantana strumming touba heiribasi mahakki mapannani hainasu khanjei. 1993 da Post Graduation loisallaba matungda mayumda lakkhi amadi mahana tamjaruba Computer Gi line duda chattaduna Eseida pukning lupsanbaduda mahakki emunggi meeosingna yamna nungaikhide. Mahakna Phubalada leiba Eina Musical Club ta yaosankhi. 1994 da mahak Tamna Musical Center da Guitarist, Composer Oina leirammi. 1995 tana Songs, Dance Drama and Cultural Association da Part time Oina yaorammi. Mahakki ehan hanba performance utkhibasi, Rudra High Schoolda Swarsati Puja Numitta oirammi, matamduda mahak lupa 50 khak fangi hai. Mahakki eseigi performance tuna emung manung pubadi ngamlamde. Leikaigi meeymanasu mahakpu karemnafam oirammi, “Guitar ama pairaga koichat chatli..” hainakasu karemnabikhi hai. Masidasu nattana mahakna eseigi lamda changsallakpa matamda, Manipuri Celluloid Film amagi esei composed toubagi matangda wari asum lirak-e. Directorduna matamdugi afaoba senior maming chatliba Nupi seisakpi amada: “Nahaan-gi sur-du taabi-rak-labra Oja?” Seisakpiduna: “Mathong maram khang-daba sur-dubu kamaina taa-doino?” Masidasu nattri, afaoba drummer amana asumna hairakkhi, “Manipurgi music artist 100% na nangbu nung-shide, ama-sung nungi esei taaningde, programme su yeng-ningde, adubu paam-badi paam-mi”. Afaoba Nupa seisakpa amanasu asumna haibikhi haina paodamalak-e Lawai macha-dubu Oja-na nak-sin-naraga chaak-ka kouridubu kari touge hairibano? Asigumba samajki meenok meisa marakta, meeyamna sak-khangba, meeyamna pamnaba seisakpa ama oirakpa haibasi, mahakki akhang kanba, thouna lingjel adunani. Asigumba meewa meisa marakta, ngasidi meeyamgi Tapta, Jayanta na Top topna Manipurgi seisakpasinggi marakta thawanmichakkum ngallare..
